Potato Pancakes

  • Time: 10 min
  • Serves: 10
Ingredients Quantity
Peeled potatoes 500g
Flour 2 tablespoons
Onion 1
Egg 1
Oil 2 tablespoons
Milk 2-3 tablespoons
Salt ¾ of a teaspoon
Pepper 1

Step 1: Grate potatoes and give the mass a good squeeze.

Step 2: Put potatoes into the large bowl, add an onion, egg, flour, salt, milk, oil and pepper.

Step 3: Heat some oil in the frying pan.

Step 4: Put some potato mass onto the frying pan with the tablespoon. Fry draniki for 2-3
minutes, then flip then and fry on the other side.

Step 5: Serve hot with sour cream.

Apple Crumble

  • Time: 40 min
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Big apples 3
Flour 300 g
Sugar 100 g
Butter 200 g
Cream a bit
Milk 2-3 tablespoons
Water 200 mg

Step 1: Slice the peeled apples.

Step 2: Pour a bit of water into the saucepan. Put the apples into the water and add 50 g of sugar.

Step 3: Stew the apples for 10 minutes.

Step 4: Mix the flour, butter and 50 g of sugar in a bowl to make the dough.

Step 5: Place the prepared apples into the baking dish and cover them with the dough.

Step 6: Bake the crumble for 30 minutes in the oven at 180 degrees.

Step 7: Serve the pie with cream, milk, juice, tea or coffee.

Brownie

  • Time: 45 min
  • Serves: 6-8
Ingredients Quantity
Dark chocolate 100 g
Flour 100 g
Sugar 1 glass
Butter 180 g
Eggs 4
Walnuts 100 g
Water 200 mg

Step 1: Melt butter and chocolate in a bain-marie. Mix everything.

Step 2: Whisk eggs and sugar with the mixer for 2-3 minutes.

Step 3: Chop walnuts with a knife and toast them a little in a dry frying pan.

Step 4: Add flour to the cooled butter and chocolate mixture and stir thoroughly.

Step 5: After that add eggs whisked with sugar and stir carefully until the mixture is homogeneous in colour.

Step 6: Turn on the oven and heat it to 200°C. The baking tray shouldn't be too big. Line the baking tray with parchment paper and pour in the mixture. Bake for 25-30 minutes.

Step 7: Cut the brownie once it has cooled down. Use a very sharp and thin knife.
